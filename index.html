My favourite hobbie is reading books

My favorite hobby is reading books. I read all kinds of good books, magazines, newspapers etc. I am to read some books for my examination. I read them with much attention. But when I get leisure or spare time I start reading other books that I like. I feel happy if I get a new book every day. I read novels, fairy tales, adventure stories, detective novels, poems, essays etc. I do not like to waste time in idle gossiping. I treat the books as my friends as they delight me even at the time of my danger and loneliness. I spend about an hour every day on reading. I enjoy very much reading any kind of good books. I also acquire knowledge from my reading. It helps me broaden my outlook. So a man must have a hobby that gives pleasure, knowledge and removes the pangs of loneliness.
